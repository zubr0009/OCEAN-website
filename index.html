What is OCEAN?

OCEAN stands for Ottawa Christian Educators and Administration Network. Our mission is to connect Christians working in the Education sector in order that they may fellowship, network, be encouraged and inspired to authentically follow Jesus by responding to the Holy Spirit wherever they work.

Who's Involved?

Christians working in Education (elementary or secondary; public, Catholic or private) in the greater Ottawa area that are:

Teachers

·

Administrators

·

Office Staff

·

Care Staff

·

EA's

·

Retired Teachers

·

Education Students

·

Occasional Teachers

·

Union Workers
